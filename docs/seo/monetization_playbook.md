# Monetization Playbook (Practical + Low Risk)

## 1) Productize the Safe Version

- Keep the open-source core.
- Offer paid add-ons:
  - team policy presets,
  - advanced rule editor,
  - enterprise support/SLA,
  - managed configuration sync.

## 2) Extension Store Funnel

- Free extension with safe defaults.
- Upsell to:
  - premium rule packs,
  - multi-workspace profiles,
  - faster support.

## 3) Consulting + Integration

- Offer setup services for teams with repetitive approval flows.
- Sell fixed-fee onboarding and maintenance.

## 4) Trust Signals

- Publish reliability stats (false-positive reductions, cooldown behavior).
- Keep changelog and incident notes transparent.
- Show security/privacy docs clearly.

## 5) What to Avoid

- Do not auto-spam communities.
- Do not claim unsupported guarantees.
- Do not bypass platform terms.
